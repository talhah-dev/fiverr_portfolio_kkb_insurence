Who We Are

KBB Insurance Group is an independent life insurance brokerage focused on helping clients find the right type of coverage, whether that's term, whole life, or universal life insurance.

We work with multiple top-rated insurance companies and take a strategic approach to every application, ensuring each client is matched with the carrier best suited for their specific situation.

About Keith Brown

Keith Brown is the founder of KBB Insurance Group. He earned degrees in Biology and Chemistry from the University of West Georgia and began his career in pharmaceutical sales, where he developed a strong understanding of health conditions, medications, and how they are evaluated.

He now applies that same knowledge to life insurance underwriting, helping clients navigate the approval process more effectively and avoid unnecessary declines.

In addition to working directly with clients, Keith trains and mentors his team on the same underwriting-driven process to ensure consistency, efficiency, and better outcomes across the board.

How We Help

Our agents specialize in matching clients with the right insurance company based on their health, medications, and overall risk profile, not guesswork.

This approach improves approval rates, avoids unnecessary delays, and ensures clients are placed with the carrier most likely to accept them from the start.

The goal is simple, get clients approved quickly, with the right coverage, at a price that makes sense.
