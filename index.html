Who We Are

KBB Insurance Group is an independent life insurance agency built around one core advantage: underwriting expertise. We don't just shop plans, we understand how insurance companies make approval decisions, allowing us to position every client correctly from the start and avoid unnecessary delays or declines.

Our team works across multiple top-rated carriers and knows exactly which companies are most favorable for different health conditions, medications, and risk profiles. This allows us to guide each client toward the option that offers the highest likelihood of approval and the most competitive rate based on their specific situation.

Unlike traditional agencies that rely on trial and error, we take a strategic approach to every application. By leveraging underwriting knowledge upfront, we reduce friction in the process and help clients secure coverage without the frustration of multiple submissions or uncertain outcomes.

Our objective is simple: match every client with the right type of coverage, whether that's term, whole life, or universal life, and when needed, secure day one coverage with no waiting period. Every recommendation is tailored to the client's goals, health profile, and budget to ensure long-term protection that actually performs when it matters most.

Our process is designed to maximize approvals, speed up decisions, and eliminate wasted applications. The result is a more efficient experience and a higher success rate for clients who want coverage done right the first time.

About Keith Brown

Keith Brown is the founder of KBB Insurance Group and specializes in life insurance underwriting and policy placement.

He holds degrees in Biology and Chemistry from the University of West Georgia and has a background in pharmaceutical sales, giving him a strong understanding of how medical conditions and medications are evaluated.

This experience allows Keith to accurately match clients with the right insurance company based on their specific health profile, helping improve approval rates and avoid unnecessary delays.

In addition to working directly with clients, Keith also trains and mentors his team on the same underwriting strategies and processes, ensuring a consistent and efficient experience across every interaction.

His focus is simple - get clients approved quickly, efficiently, and with coverage that fits their needs and budget.
